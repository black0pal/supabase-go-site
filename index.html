Core Features

Everything you need, on the go

Project Management

View and switch between all your Supabase projects. Monitor status, region, and activity at a glance.

SQL Editor

Run custom SQL queries directly from your phone. Built for quick lookups, fixes, and lightweight scripting.

Table Browser

Browse your database tables, view row data, and inspect your schema without opening a browser.

Storage Management

Navigate your storage buckets, view uploaded files, and manage your project assets from anywhere.

Biometric Security

Your tokens never leave your device. FaceID and TouchID lock keeps your credentials secure at all times.

Multi-Profile Support

Switch between multiple Supabase accounts instantly. Perfect for developers managing client projects.
