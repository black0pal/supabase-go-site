Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

At Supabase Go, we prioritize your privacy and the security of your developer credentials. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

We believe in absolute data sovereignty. Supabase Go is designed to be a "Zero-Server" architecture regarding your database credentials:

  • Personal Access Tokens (PATs): Your Supabase PATs are stored locally on your device using secure, encrypted storage. They are NEVER sent to our servers.
  • Database Content: We do not see, store, or intercept any of the data from your Supabase projects. The app connects directly from your device to the Supabase API.

2. Data We Collect

To provide subscription services and improve the app, we collect minimal data:

  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. This involves an anonymous identifier to track your "Pro" or "Business" status across devices.
  • Diagnostics: We may collect anonymous crash reports and performance data to improve the application's stability.

3. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Local Encryption: All sensitive credentials are stored in the device's secure enclave (Keychain on iOS).
  • Biometric Lock: Users can enable FaceID or TouchID to prevent unauthorized local access to the application.

4. Third-Party Services

Supabase Go interacts with:

  • Supabase API: To manage your projects.
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing and app distribution.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription logic.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
