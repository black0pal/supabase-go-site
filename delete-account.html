Account Deletion Policy

At Supabase Go, we believe you should have full control over your data. Because our application is primarily client-side, "deleting your account" is a straightforward process handled directly within the app.

How to delete your data

1

Remove Profiles: Open the Supabase Go app, go to the Profile/Settings section, and delete each of your connected Supabase profiles. This immediately wipes all stored Personal Access Tokens (PATs) from your device's secure storage.

2

Cancel Subscriptions: If you have a Pro or Business subscription, you must cancel it via the Apple App Store settings on your iPhone. Deleting the app does not automatically cancel a paid subscription.

3

Request RevenueCat Deletion: While we don't store your database data, RevenueCat stores an anonymous identifier for your subscription. If you wish to have this identifier deleted, please email us at black0pal@yahoo.com with your request.

Data Retention

Once you remove a profile from the app, all associated tokens and cached metadata are permanently purged from your device. We do not keep backups of your credentials on any server.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need assistance with the deletion process, please reach out to us at black0pal@yahoo.com.
